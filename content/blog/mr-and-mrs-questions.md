There's a reason the mr and mrs game is one of the most iconic wedding entertainment ideas out there. It's simple, it's hilarious, and it has a magical way of revealing things about the couple that even their closest friends didn't know.

Whether you're planning a hen do, a wedding reception, or even a chilled engagement party, a well-crafted mr and mrs quiz is guaranteed to get everyone laughing — and maybe a little red in the face. If you're still in the early stages of planning, make sure to check out our <a href="/blog/wedding-checklist/">ultimate wedding planning checklist</a> to keep everything on track.

We've pulled together 101 of the best mr and mrs game questions that cover everything from the adorably romantic to the downright cheeky. Pick your favourites, mix and match, and watch the sparks (and the squirming) fly.

## How to Play the Mr and Mrs Game

Before we dive into the questions, here's a quick refresher on how the mr and mrs game works:

<table>
<tr><th>How to play</th></tr>
<tr><td>One half of the couple sits facing the audience — the other is backstage, on video call, or has pre-recorded their answers</td></tr>
<tr><td>A host reads out each question and the person in the hot seat gives their answer</td></tr>
<tr><td>Reveal what the partner said — every match earns a point, every mismatch earns a laugh</td></tr>
</table>

<table>
<tr><th>What we love most</th></tr>
<tr><td>Film the whole thing — you'll want to relive those facial expressions for years</td></tr>
<tr><td>Use "him" and "her" paddles for visual answers that make brilliant photos</td></tr>
<tr><td>Pick a confident host who can read the room and add a bit of commentary</td></tr>
</table>

## Classic Mr & Mrs Questions

<img src="/images/blog-mr-mrs-classic-2.webp" alt="Couple laughing together during classic mr and mrs game at reception" />

Let's warm things up with the essentials. These are the tried-and-tested mr and mrs questions that every quiz needs — perfect for settling nerves and getting the crowd invested.

1. What's your partner's all-time favourite film to rewatch on a lazy Sunday?
2. If they could only eat one meal for the rest of their life, what would it be?
3. What song do they know every single word to?
4. What was their very first job — and did they hate it?
5. How many sugars do they take in their tea (or do they commit the crime of not drinking tea at all)?
6. What's the one thing they absolutely cannot leave the house without?
7. If they won the lottery tomorrow, what's the first thing they'd buy?
8. What size shoe do they wear?
9. What's their go-to takeaway order on a Friday night?
10. What's their middle name — and do they secretly hate it?

You'll be surprised how many partners fumble even the basics. These warm-up rounds are pure gold for getting everyone invested before the real chaos begins.

21. What's the strangest thing they've ever eaten — on purpose?
22. If they were a cartoon character, who would they be?
23. What's the most questionable outfit they've ever worn in public?
24. What household chore do they dramatically avoid at all costs?
25. If their life had a theme tune, what would it be?
26. What's the most useless talent or party trick they have?
27. What's the one thing in their wardrobe you'd happily set fire to?
28. What do they do that they think is cool but absolutely isn't?
29. If a film was made about their life, which actor would play them (and which actor would *they* say should play them)?

31. What habit of theirs would you pay good money to make disappear?
32. What's the most dramatic they've ever been about a minor inconvenience?
33. What's their signature move when they're trying to get out of trouble with you?

Nothing brings a room together quite like watching someone try to defend their questionable life choices in front of all their loved ones.

## The Memory Test — How Well Do You Actually Remember?

Time to find out if your partner was actually paying attention during all those milestone moments. These mr and mrs game questions are a proper test of the memory banks.

44. What colour were they wearing when you first met?
45. What was the first gift they ever bought you?
46. Where exactly did your first kiss happen?
47. What did you do on your very first Valentine's Day together?
48. What was the first thing you cooked for each other (and was it edible)?
49. What film did you see together on your first proper cinema date?
50. What was the first holiday you went on as a couple, and what went wrong?
51. Who texted first after your initial meeting?
52. What was the name of the restaurant or pub where you had your first date?
53. What did they say that made you laugh for the very first time?

The memory round is always a crowd favourite because the confident answers that turn out to be completely wrong are peak entertainment.

Quick-fire, opinion-based questions that sound innocent but somehow always spark a debate. These are fantastic for keeping the pace of your mr and mrs game snappy.

86. Toilet roll: over or under?
87. Sleeping with socks on: acceptable or absolutely not?
88. Pineapple on pizza: delicious or a crime against food?
89. Christmas film of choice: Love Actually or Die Hard?
90. Ideal holiday: beach lounger or city adventure?
91. Morning person or night owl — and does the other one suffer for it?
92. Dogs or cats, and is this a dealbreaker?
93. Ketchup in the fridge or in the cupboard?
94. The big light on, or lamps only?
95. Arrive early to everything, or fashionably late every single time?

These rapid-fire rounds are brilliant for keeping the energy high between the deeper questions. They're also surprisingly revealing — if you can't agree on the big light, what hope is there?

## Tips for Hosting the Perfect Mr & Mrs Quiz

You've got the questions — now here's how to make the whole thing run like a dream.

<table>
<tr><th>How to host</th></tr>
<tr><td>Pick 20–25 questions max — the full 101 is a menu, not a to-do list</td></tr>
<tr><td>Mix your categories — alternate between funny, sweet, and cheeky to keep the energy dynamic</td></tr>
<tr><td>Get the answers in advance — film one partner beforehand, then play the clips during the live quiz</td></tr>
<tr><td>Use paddles or whiteboards — it's visual, it's fun, and it makes for brilliant photos</td></tr>
<tr><td>Appoint a confident host who can read the room and keep things moving</td></tr>
<tr><td>Record everything — this is the kind of content you'll want to watch on every anniversary</td></tr>
</table>

<table>
<tr><th>What we love most</th></tr>
<tr><td>The mr and mrs game needs zero budget — just brilliant questions and two people in love</td></tr>

<tr><td>It's the kind of entertainment that everyone — from the bridesmaids to the grandparents — can enjoy</td></tr>
</table>

The beauty of the mr and mrs game is its simplicity. You don't need a massive budget, elaborate props, or hours of planning. Just a brilliant set of questions, two people who love each other, and a room full of friends and family ready to laugh.

Whether you use these mr and mrs quiz questions at a hen party, stag do, engagement bash, or the wedding reception itself — they're guaranteed to create the kind of moments that everyone talks about for years.

So grab your paddles, warm up your best hosting voice, and let the quiz begin. May the best partner win.
