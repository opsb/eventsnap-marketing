If you've been dreaming of a destination wedding filled with sunshine, culture, passion, and unforgettable scenery, Spain, especially Andalusia, should be at the top of your list.

Andalusia is famous for its 320+ days of sun, warm hospitality, rich food culture, flamenco energy, whitewashed villages, olive groves stretching for miles, and some of Europe's most spectacular venues. From the glamorous shores of Marbella, to the dramatic mountains of Granada, to the timeless charm of Seville, this Spanish region truly has it all.

Whether you prefer a luxury villa by the sea, a romantic cortijo surrounded by nature, or a historic castle dripping in Spanish charm, Andalusia offers something for every type of couple.

Below are 15 of the most breathtaking wedding venues across Málaga, Cádiz, Seville, Granada, and more.

## The Best Wedding Venues in Andalusia

### 1. Villa Luisa — Seville

![Villa Luisa](/images/venue-01-villa-luisa.webp)

A historic palace in the heart of Seville, Villa Luisa blends regal elegance with lush botanical gardens and stunning architectural details.

**Airport:** Sevilla Airport, ~20 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Iconic early 20th-century architecture
- Palm-filled gardens for dreamy outdoor ceremonies
- Elegant indoor halls for year-round weddings

**Perfect for:** Elegant city weddings, historic venues, timeless celebrations.

### 2. Villa Las Sirenas — Sotogrande

![Villa Las Sirenas](/images/venue-02-las-sirenas.webp)

Overlooking the coastline of Southern Spain, this luxury villa offers privacy, modern design, and panoramic sea views.

**Airport:** Gibraltar Airport, ~30 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Infinity-style terraces with sea views
- Boutique-hotel luxury
- Perfect for multi-day celebrations

**Perfect for:** Luxury villa weddings, intimate groups, modern sophistication.

### 3. La Chumbera — Granada

![La Chumbera](/images/venue-03-la-chumbera.webp)

Set on the hillside facing the Alhambra, La Chumbera delivers unbeatable views, atmospheric gardens, and Moorish-inspired architecture.

**Airport:** Granada Airport, ~25 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Unmatched Alhambra backdrop
- Magical evening atmosphere
- Terraces ideal for sunset dining

**Perfect for:** Romantic, cultural weddings, Granada lovers, unique backdrops.

### 4. Finca Molino del Duque — Marbella

![Finca Molino del Duque](/images/venue-04-molino-del-duque.webp)

This restored Andalusian mill offers rustic charm infused with luxury, surrounded by gardens and Andalusian countryside.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~50 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Authentic Andalusian finca aesthetic
- Indoor/outdoor flexibility
- Tranquil countryside setting

**Perfect for:** Rustic luxury weddings, boho chic, outdoor ceremonies.

### 5. Huerta del Conde — Málaga

![Huerta del Conde](/images/venue-05-huerta-del-conde.webp)

A traditional estate surrounded by olive trees and rolling hills, ideal for couples seeking privacy close to Málaga. An absolutely stunning villa.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~30 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Gorgeous Andalusian courtyard
- Sunset views over the countryside
- Ideal for relaxed, elegant celebrations

**Perfect for:** Nature lovers, traditional Spanish vibes, countryside weddings.

### 6. Finca Concepción — Marbella

![Finca Concepción](/images/venue-06-finca-concepcion.webp)

A lush, tropical-style venue with towering palms, blooming gardens, and open-air spaces that create a Mediterranean oasis.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~45 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Tropical garden atmosphere
- Perfect for large weddings
- Magical evening lighting

**Perfect for:** Garden weddings, glamorous events, Marbella celebrations.

### 7. Finca el Cañuelo — Tarifa / Cádiz

![Finca el Cañuelo](/images/venue-07-finca-canuelo.webp)

A whitewashed Andalusian estate overlooking the most stunning beaches of Southern Spain, with a relaxed boho feel.

**Airport:** Gibraltar Airport, ~40 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Terraces with jaw-dropping sea views
- Laid-back Andalusian charm
- Close to Tarifa's beaches

**Perfect for:** Boho weddings, beach lovers, sunset ceremonies.

### 8. Castillo de la Monclova — Seville Province

![Castillo de la Monclova](/images/venue-08-castillo-monclova.webp)

A 14th-century castle surrounded by olive groves, offering fairytale courtyards, towers, and historical magic.

**Airport:** Sevilla Airport, ~1 hour

**Why we love it:**

- Majestic architecture
- Endless photo locations
- Perfect for grand entrances

**Perfect for:** Fairytale weddings, big celebrations, castle lovers.

### 9. Hacienda Faín Viejo — Arcos de la Frontera, Cádiz

![Hacienda Faín Viejo](/images/venue-09-hacienda-fain.webp)

A breathtaking historic estate surrounded by olive groves, lakes, and lush gardens, Hacienda Faín Viejo feels like a secluded paradise. With its Andalusian architecture, courtyards, and exquisite interiors, it offers an unforgettable blend of heritage and natural beauty.

**Airport:** Jerez Airport, ~35 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Vast gardens and water features
- Beautiful antique-filled interiors
- Ideal for multi-day celebrations with on-site accommodation

**Perfect for:** Romantic estate weddings, heritage lovers, exclusive weekend events.

### 10. Cortijo San Francisco — Estepona

![Cortijo San Francisco](/images/venue-10-cortijo-san-francisco.webp)

Set in the peaceful Andalusian countryside just minutes from Estepona, Cortijo San Francisco is a beautifully restored estate that captures the essence of traditional southern Spain. With whitewashed walls, rustic courtyards, blooming bougainvillea, and panoramic views over rolling hills, it offers an intimate and authentic setting for couples seeking a relaxed yet elegant wedding atmosphere.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~45 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Warm rustic charm
- Gorgeous arches and courtyards
- Perfect sunsets

**Perfect for:** Rustic weddings, intimate groups, traditional aesthetics.

### 11. Cortijo Pedro Jiménez — Ronda Area

![Cortijo Pedro Jiménez](/images/venue-11-cortijo-pedro-jimenez.webp)

Set on expansive land near Ronda, this cortijo blends elegant minimalism with countryside tranquility, offering wide-open views, clean architectural lines, and peaceful outdoor spaces surrounded by olive groves and rolling hills.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~1 hour 15 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Clean, modern-meets-rustic design
- Surrounded by mountains and fields
- Ideal for open-air dining

**Perfect for:** Minimalist weddings, countryside escapes, slow weddings.

### 12. Retiro Sambana — Cádiz Province

![Retiro Sambana](/images/venue-12-retiro-sambana.webp)

A peaceful hacienda immersed in nature, offering a relaxed, bohemian atmosphere with a traditional Cádiz touch.

**Airport:** Jerez Airport, ~35 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Surrounded by nature
- Boho-elegant setting
- Charming pool area

**Perfect for:** Relaxed weddings, weekend celebrations, outdoor lovers.

### 13. Finca Monasterio — Sotogrande

![Finca Monasterio](/images/venue-13-finca-monasterio.webp)

This boutique hotel and former monastery combines historic charm with luxury amenities, offering atmospheric cloisters, serene courtyards, and beautifully designed rooms that make it ideal for hosting guests on-site and creating a full weekend wedding experience.

**Airport:** Gibraltar Airport, ~25 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Courtyards with Andalusian tiles
- Rooms for guests
- Private, serene surroundings

**Perfect for:** Destination weddings, full-weekend stays, elegant celebrations.

### 14. Finca Cortesin — Casares (Málaga)

![Finca Cortesin](/images/venue-14-finca-cortesin.webp)

One of Spain's most luxurious resorts, offering immaculate gardens, world-class service, and Mediterranean sophistication.

**Airport:** Malaga Airport, ~1 hour

**Why we love it:**

- Ultra-luxury experience
- Magazine-worthy ceremony spots
- Perfect for chic, high-end weddings

**Perfect for:** Luxury weddings, glamorous couples, 5-star experiences.

### 15. La Esperanza — Granada

![La Esperanza](/images/venue-15-la-esperanza.webp)

A romantic estate surrounded by mountains and lush greenery, offering boutique luxury and serene privacy.

**Airport:** Granada Airport, ~35 mins

**Why we love it:**

- Mountain views and gardens
- Elegant villas for staying on-site
- Peaceful retreat close to Granada

**Perfect for:** Intimate weddings, nature lovers, romantic escapes.
