Tips for Choosing the Perfect Wedding Hashtag

Check Uniqueness

Search your hashtag on Instagram and TikTok before committing — make sure it's not already widely used by someone else.

Keep It Simple

Choose a hashtag that's easy to spell and remember. Avoid numbers, underscores, or anything guests might misspell.

Tell Your Guests

Print your hashtag on table cards, menus, or a sign at the entrance. The more visible it is, the more photos you'll collect!
